How To Make Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients:

  • Meat: 1lb of sweet Italian sausage or 3/4lb lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: 1/2 cup of minced onion and two crushed cloves of garlic.
  • Tomato Products: 1 canned crushed tomatoes, 2 canned tomato sauce, and two canned tomato paste.
  • Water: 1/2 cup of water.
  • Sugar: 2 tablespoons of white sugar.
  • Spice and Seasonings: 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley. 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves. 1 ½ teaspoons salt. 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning. ½ teaspoon fennel seeds. ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles: 12 lasagna noodle sheets. (Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.)
  • Cheese: 16 ounces ricotta cheese, ¾ pound sliced mozzarella cheese, and ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Egg: 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Cook the meat sauce.
    • Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 ½ hours, stirring occasionally.
  2. Cook the noodles.
    • Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
    • In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
    • To assemble, spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 ½ cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
    • Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
    • Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
    • Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
